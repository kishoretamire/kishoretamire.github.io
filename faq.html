Frequently Asked Questions

Online Text Editor is a free online tool that helps you edit and format text. It's designed to save time and ensure consistency in your text formatting needs.

Yes, all text processing happens directly in your browser. We don't store or transmit your text to any servers. Your data remains private and secure on your device.

Title Case capitalizes the first letter of each major word (e.g., "The Quick Brown Fox"), while Sentence case only capitalizes the first letter of the first word and proper nouns (e.g., "The quick brown fox").

While Online Text Editor requires an internet connection to load initially, once loaded, it can work offline. Your text is also saved locally, so you won't lose your work if you close the browser.

There's no strict limit, but for optimal performance, we recommend processing text in chunks of up to 100,000 characters at a time.

The Text Formatter tab provides various text modification options. Simply paste your text, select the desired formatting option (remove line breaks, extra spaces, etc.), and click the corresponding button. You can then copy or download the formatted text.

Yes, Online Text Editor includes an undo feature. After making any changes to your text, you can click the 'Undo' button to revert to the previous state. The tool maintains a history of your recent changes.

'Remove Line Breaks' combines all lines into a single continuous line, while 'Remove Empty Lines' only removes blank lines while preserving the paragraph structure. Choose based on your formatting needs.

Yes, Online Text Editor is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The interface automatically adjusts to provide the best experience on your device's screen size.

After converting your text, click the 'Download Text' button. Your text will be downloaded as a .txt file that you can open with any text editor.

Currently, you can use standard keyboard shortcuts like Ctrl+C (copy), Ctrl+V (paste), and Ctrl+Z (undo) within the text area. We're working on adding more keyboard shortcuts in future updates.

Our word count tool accurately counts words by identifying text separated by spaces and punctuation. It also handles multiple spaces and line breaks correctly. The character count includes all characters, including spaces and punctuation.
