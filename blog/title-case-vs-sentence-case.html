When to Use Title Case vs Sentence Case

Published on January 15, 2024

Understanding Title Case

Title case is commonly used for headlines, book titles, and article headings. In title case, you capitalize:

  • The first and last words
  • All major words (nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs)
  • Articles (a, an, the) when they are the first or last word

When to Use Sentence Case

Sentence case is more common in body text and some modern web designs. In sentence case:

  • Only capitalize the first word
  • Capitalize proper nouns
  • Keep everything else lowercase
